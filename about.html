Crafting Wine Stories Since 1994

At Afta 94, we believe wine is more than a beverage—it's a conversation between land, history, and heart. Our journey began in 1994 with a vision to bring the world’s most soulful vintages to every glass. We curate exceptional wines with respect for terroir, tradition, and the artistry of winemaking.

Whether you’re a seasoned sommelier or a curious explorer, Afta 94 invites you to savor wines that speak in notes of elegance, balance, and legacy.
